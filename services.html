Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does it take to build a website?
Timeline varies based on complexity. A simple business website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce sites or web applications may take 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you accept payment in Ethiopian Birr?
Yes! We accept payment in Ethiopian Birr via CBE, Telebirr, and other local payment methods. We also accept international payments via Visa/Mastercard.
Do you provide hosting services?
Yes! We offer reliable hosting packages with 99.9% uptime guarantee, regular backups, and 24/7 monitoring. Hosting can be purchased monthly or annually.
Can you integrate Telebirr or CBE payment?
Absolutely! We specialize in integrating Ethiopian payment methods including CBE, Telebirr, and M-PESA. We also support international payment gateways.
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. All packages include 24/7 support and monitoring.
Can we meet at your 4 Kilo office?
Certainly! We're located at 4 Kilo, Addis Ababa, opposite Addis Ababa University. We welcome in-person meetings by appointment. You can also reach us via phone or video call.
