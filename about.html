Our Story

Founded in 2026 in the heart of Addis Ababa, ethioExpertise began with a simple mission: to help Ethiopian businesses establish a powerful online presence. What started as a one-person operation in 4 Kilo has grown into a team of passionate developers, designers, and digital strategists.

We believe that great websites are more than just code – they're tools that drive growth, engage customers, and tell your brand's story. Every project we undertake is approached with creativity, technical excellence, and a focus on results.

Today, we've helped over 100 clients across Ethiopia and beyond achieve their digital goals, and we're just getting started.
