Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly are you located in Addis Ababa?

We are located in 4 Kilo, Addis Ababa, directly opposite the Addis Ababa University Faculty of Technology. Our office is easily accessible by public transport and there's parking available for clients.

What are your response times for inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, please call our phone number directly.

Do you offer services outside Ethiopia?

Yes! While our physical office is in Addis Ababa, we serve clients globally through remote consultations and project delivery. We have clients in the US, Europe, and across Africa.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the right team member is available to assist you. You can call us or use the contact form to set up a meeting.
